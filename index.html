About Us

Founded in 2010, Caramel Cloud is a family-owned bakery known for its delicious, freshly baked cookies and cakes. Our mission is to bring happiness to every customer through high-quality, handcrafted desserts made with locally sourced ingredients. What sets us apart is our commitment to freshness, custom creations for special occasions, and a deep connection to our local community. Whether you're looking for a classic treat or a custom cake, Sweet Treats Bakery is here to make your day sweeter!

About Caramel Cloud


Welcome to Caramel Cloud, where every bite is a celebration of flavor and tradition. Established in 2010, our bakery began as a small, family-run business with a passion for crafting exceptional cookies and cakes. Over the years, Sweet Treats Bakery has grown into a cherished local gem, renowned for its dedication to quality and its warm, inviting atmosphere. At Caramel Cloud, our mission is to create moments of joy through our delectable baked goods. We believe that great food has the power to bring people together and make everyday moments special.Each batch of cookies, cake, or pastry is crafted with care and attention to detail, ensuring that every treat is fresh, flavorful, and made with love. At Sweet Treats Bakery, we’re more than just a bakery – we’re a place where sweet moments are made.
